# Microsoft 365 Developer Program

## Overview
The Microsoft 365 Developer Program's Instant Sandbox is a fully pre-configured E5 developer environment that provisions in minutes, pre-loaded with 16 fictitious sample users, Microsoft Teams data packs, Microsoft Graph and Office Add-ins data, and pre-configured custom apps.

What's new is the addition of add-on commerce capability — you can now purchase additional services, including Microsoft Copilot, directly from within your sandbox environment. This enables developers to test and build against paid add-on services without needing a separate production subscription.

The Instant Sandbox remains the faster alternative to the Configurable Sandbox and includes:
•	16 fictitious sample users
•	Microsoft Teams data packs
•	Microsoft Graph and Office Add-ins data
•	Pre-configured custom apps
•	New: Ability to purchase additional services (including Microsoft Copilot) directly from your sandbox

> **Important:** - You will not be charged for the free E5 developer sandbox. Billing details are required only for account verification and to enable future add-on purchases.
